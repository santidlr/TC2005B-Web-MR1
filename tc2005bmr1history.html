Foundation

For a couple years, the owners, executives, and manager started their venture into the world of football as a small club working from a shared training site (an iPad), playing in the lower divisions of Mexican football. In 2014 though, they brought their venture to the big stage, where all the top clubs played, the top flight of national football at the time, the PS4. 4 years of glory then came upon DeLirious FC.


The club fell apart after a disagreement with the regulating international football body, but in 2022 made its return. Since then, the club has flourished into what we know now.
